# GranWatch — App Store Age Rating Questionnaire Answers

**Correct rating: 4+ (Apple) / Everyone (Google Play)**
GranWatch contains no restricted content of any kind. It is a family coordination and eldercare app — no violence, no adult content, no gambling, no medical advice.

---

## Apple App Store — Age Rating Questionnaire

Apple's questionnaire is completed in App Store Connect under your app's listing. For each category, select the frequency level. GranWatch answers are all **None** or **No**.

### Content Descriptors

| Category | Answer | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Cartoon or Fantasy Violence | **None** | No violence of any kind |
| Realistic Violence | **None** | No violence of any kind |
| Prolonged Graphic or Sadistic Realistic Violence | **None** | No violence of any kind |
| Profanity or Crude Humour | **None** | No profanity, no crude language |
| Mature/Suggestive Themes | **None** | No suggestive content |
| Horror/Fear Themes | **None** | No scary, disturbing, or horror content |
| Medical/Treatment Information | **None** | GranWatch includes a medication and appointment logging feature, but it is a family organiser — not a medical advice or diagnosis tool. It logs user-entered information for family coordination purposes only. No medical advice is given, no diagnoses are made, no treatment is recommended. This is correctly rated None, not Infrequent/Mild. |
| Alcohol, Tobacco, or Drug Use or References | **None** | No references to substances of any kind |
| Gambling and Contests | **None** | No gambling, no simulated gambling, no contests |
| Sexual Content or Nudity | **None** | No sexual content of any kind |
| Graphic Sexual Content and Nudity | **None** | No sexual content of any kind |
| Unrestricted Web Access | **No** | The app does not provide a browser or unrestricted web browsing capability |

### Result

All answers are None / No. Apple will assign the rating **4+**.

### Where to enter this in App Store Connect

1. Log in to App Store Connect → My Apps → GranWatch
2. Select your app version (the one under review or in preparation)
3. In the left sidebar under "App Information", click **Age Rating**
4. Click "Edit" and work through each category
5. Select **None** for every content descriptor
6. Select **No** for Unrestricted Web Access
7. Apple will confirm the rating as **4+** at the bottom of the page
8. Save and return to your app listing

## Google Play — Content Rating Questionnaire (IARC System)

Google Play uses the IARC (International Age Rating Coalition) system. The questionnaire is completed in the Google Play Console under your app's content rating section.

### Where to find it

Google Play Console → Your App → Policy → App Content → Content Rating → Start Questionnaire

### Category: App Type

Select: **Apps** (not Games)

Sub-category: **Reference / News / Social Communication** is the closest fit, but "Social" or "Communication" also works. The category affects which sub-questions you see. For GranWatch, select the category closest to "Family" or "Social" — it does not materially affect the rating given all answers are None.

### Questionnaire Answers

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Does your app contain any violence? | **No** |
| Does your app contain scary content? | **No** |
| Does your app contain sexual content? | **No** |
| Does your app contain profanity or crude humour? | **No** |
| Does your app contain controlled substances (drugs, alcohol, tobacco)? | **No** |
| Does your app contain references to gambling? | **No** |
| Does your app allow users to share information with others? | **Yes** — families share visit logs and notes with each other within a private family group. This is not public social media. |
| Does your app allow users to interact with others online? | **Yes** — family members coordinate within a private group. |
| Does your app contain user-generated content? | **Yes** — users log visit notes and care schedule entries. Content is private to the family group. |

### Note on User Interaction

Answering Yes to user interaction and sharing does not increase the content rating — it triggers a disclosure label ("Users Interact", "Shares Info") that appears on the Play Store listing. This is accurate and expected for a family coordination app. It is not a negative — users understand the app is a shared tool.

### Result

With the above answers, Google Play IARC will assign: **Everyone** (rated for all ages, 3+ in some regional IARC mappings).

The Play Store listing will display:
- **Rating: Everyone**
- **Content: Users Interact, Shares Info** (informational labels, not warnings)

### Where to enter this in Google Play Console

1. Google Play Console → GranWatch → Policy → App Content
2. Click "Content Rating" → "Start questionnaire"
3. Select App type: Apps
4. Select category (choose Social, Communication, or Reference)
5. Answer each question as above
6. Review the generated rating — confirm "Everyone" appears
7. Apply rating to your app
