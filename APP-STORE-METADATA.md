### App Store Description (4000 chars max)

```
Is Gran okay? When did anyone last visit?

GranWatch answers that question — for every member of the family, at a glance, without anyone having to ask.

A colour-coded status ring tells you everything. Green means Gran was visited recently. Yellow means it's been a while. Red means the family needs to act — and everyone gets a private nudge.

No nagging group chats. No guilt. Just a quiet, shared awareness that keeps the whole family connected to the people who matter most.

─────────────────────────

HOW IT WORKS

1. Add Gran's profile
Create a profile with her name and photo. Set how often she should be visited — once a week, twice a month, whatever works for your family. The ring starts counting.

2. Invite the family
Share a link or invite code. Everyone joins the same profile in seconds. No app download required for family members — they can join straight from their browser.

3. Log visits, stay green
After each visit, tap "Log a Visit". The ring resets to green. If it turns yellow or red, the family gets a gentle nudge — privately, without anyone being called out publicly.

That's it. No complex setup. No learning curve.

─────────────────────────

BUILT FOR FAMILIES WHO LIVE APART

Whether you're across town or across the world, GranWatch keeps the whole family on the same page.

Overseas Filipino workers checking in on parents back home. South African families spread across cities. Indian diaspora in the UK and Canada. Adult children juggling jobs and their own kids while trying to stay connected to ageing parents.

GranWatch was built for exactly these situations — when love is there but logistics are hard.

─────────────────────────

FEATURES

• Visual status ring — green, yellow, or red, updated in real time
• Family alerts — automatic notifications when Gran hasn't been visited in too long
• Visit log — a running record of who visited, when, and how it went
• Planned visits — claim a date so the family knows it's covered
• Birthday reminders — never miss Gran's special day
• Up to 20 family members — invite the whole family with a single link
• Works on any device — phone, tablet, or computer, no app required

─────────────────────────

GRAN+ (Optional upgrade, shared by the family)

• Wellbeing check-ins — visitors rate Gran's mood after each visit (1–5)
• Visit photos and notes — capture memories, share updates
• Multiple gran profiles — watch over more than one family elder
• Custom alert threshold — set exactly how many days before alerts fire
• Full visit history and insights

Gran+ starts from R39/month — and the whole family can split the cost, so it works out to just a few rand each.

─────────────────────────

PRIVACY FIRST

GranWatch never shares your data with third parties. Visit alerts go only to family members on the profile — not publicly. Profile photos are stored securely on Cloudflare. You can delete your account and all your data at any time, directly from the app.

─────────────────────────

Free to start. No credit card needed. One gran profile, forever free.

Download GranWatch and make sure Gran is never forgotten. 💛
```
